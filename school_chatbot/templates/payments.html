Mobile Money Instructions

Create a payment request first. The system will generate a payment reference number such as PAY-YYYYMMDDHHMMSS-ID.

MTN Mobile Money

  1. Dial *165# on your MTN line.
  2. Select Payments.
  3. Select School Fees or Pay Bill.
  4. Enter the school payment details requested by the menu.
  5. Enter the amount in UGX.
  6. Enter your generated payment reference number.
  7. Confirm the details and enter your Mobile Money PIN.
  8. Keep the MTN transaction message for your records.

Airtel Money Uganda

  1. Dial *185# on your Airtel line.
  2. Select Payments.
  3. Select School Fees or Pay Bill.
  4. Enter the school payment details requested by the menu.
  5. Enter the amount in UGX.
  6. Enter your generated payment reference number.
  7. Confirm the details and enter your Airtel Money PIN.
  8. Keep the Airtel transaction message for your records.

After paying, share your mobile money transaction ID with Accounts if confirmation is needed.

Contact accounts at {{ school_info.phone }} for help.
