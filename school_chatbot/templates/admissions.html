Admission Fee Payment

Create a payment request in the parent portal first, then enter the payment reference and mobile money transaction ID here.

1. Create Reference

  1. Login to the parent portal.
  2. Open Payments.
  3. Enter the admission fee amount.
  4. Create the payment request.

2. MTN Mobile Money

  1. Dial *165#.
  2. Select Payments.
  3. Select School Fees or Pay Bill.
  4. Enter amount and reference.
  5. Confirm with your PIN.

3. Airtel Money

  1. Dial *185#.
  2. Select Payments.
  3. Select School Fees or Pay Bill.
  4. Enter amount and reference.
  5. Confirm with your PIN.
